DU Admission 2021: Delhi University Application, Exam Dates, Eligibility

DU Admission 2021 will be organized to offer admission in various UG and PG courses. University offers admission in the field of engineering, management and law. Admission will be provided based on both merit and entrance examination. Entrance examination will be conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). In medical courses admission will be offered based on NEET and in management courses it will be done based on CAT scores. Candidates can read this complete article to know complete details about DU Admission 2021 including dates, application form, eligibility etc.

DU Application Form 2021

Candidates can check details about DU 2021 application form:

  • Application form for all courses will be released through online mode.
  • Candidates will be able to fill their application form from the first week of March 2021.
  • For merit based UG courses application form will be released in the third week of May 2021.
  • Registration process will contain steps i.e. filling of application form, document uploads, payment of fee etc.
  • Upload the scanned images of photograph and signature in the JPG/ JPEG format.
  • Candidates can fill their application form till the last week of March 2021. For merit based UG courses form will be available till last week of June 2021.
  • Candidates will not need to send any hardcopy of the application form by post.
  • Candidates must take printout of filled application form and keep it safe for other admission procedure.

DU Admission Eligibility

Candidates must go through the DU 2021 eligibility criteria before filling the application form:

UG Courses:

B.Tech (Information Technology and Mathematical Innovation):

  • Qualification: Candidates must have passed 10th and 12th examination or its equivalent with PCM and English subjects.
  • Marks: Candidates must secure minimum of 60% in their qualifying examination.

B.EL.ED (Bachelors of Elementary Education):

  • Qualification: Candidates must pass 10+2 examination or its equivalent exam from any recognized board.
  • Marks Criteria: Candidates must have secures minimum of 50% marks in their 12th class.

B.Sc. in Physical Education, Health Education and Sports:

  • Qualification: Candidates must pass intermediate examination from any recognized board.
  • Marks: Candidates must obtain 45% aggregate of marks in their 12th class.

PG Courses:

M.Com Course:

  • Qualification: Candidates must have passed B.A., BBA, B.Com or its equivalent from any recognized institute.
  • Marks: Candidates must have secured minimum of 50% marks (45% for OBC, 40% for SC/ ST) in their qualifying examination.

LLM Course:

  • Qualification: Candidates must have LLB degree of 3 or 5 years from any recognized institute.
  • Marks: Candidates must secure 50% marks (45% for OBC and 40% for SC/ ST) in their qualifying examination.

M.Tech Courses:

  • Qualification: Candidates must have passed B.Tech/ B.E. or its equivalent degree from any recognized board.
  • Marks: Candidates must secure 60% marks in their qualifying degree.

DU Admission 2021 Cut Off

University will release DU Admission cut off for both merit based and entrance based admission. Cut off will be released through online mode on the website. Candidates must have to obtain minimum cut of marks to be eligible for admission.

Cut off will be prepared based on various factors including availability of seats, pervious year cut off, entrance exam scores etc. University will release different cut off lists for UG and PG courses.

Cut off will be released for candidates who will be eligible for admission. If candidates will not be selected in first cut off list they can try in second or third cut off list.

DU Admission 2021

After qualifying of entrance examination candidates will be selected for the counselling process. Candidates will have to register for counselling through online mode. Based on the cut off marks candidates will have to participate in the interview and group discussion rounds.

Candidates are advised to bring their original documents for verification process. It will be necessary to bring original documents or photocopies in the counselling process. Candidates will also need to submit counselling fee.

After completing all the procedures seats will be allotted to the candidates. Seats will be allotted to the candidates based on factors i.e. interview/ group discussion, verification of documents etc.

Here we have mentioned the documents which will be needed for DU Admission 2021:

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • Migration certificate
  • Graduation mark sheets
  • Transfer certificate
  • Category certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Valid ID proof
  • Passport size photographs etc.
